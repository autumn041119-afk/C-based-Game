                    < Gameplay Introduction >                         
                                                                      
                                                                      
The game starts with you being kidnapped by a mysterious man and      
now you have to start your  escape from Antarctica and reach your     
destination by choosing a route on the world map! Each continent      
has its own unique level waiting for you, in each level you need      
to clear the level within the time limit, when the time is up if      
you don't clear the level, the assassin sent by the mysterious man    
will come after you, when the assassin and you are in the same        
continent means that the game is lost. When you reach your destination
and pass the level successfully you will win, good luck!
