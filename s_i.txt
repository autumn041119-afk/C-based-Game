                    < Introduction to Symbols >                    
                                                                   
     "*"                 Escape routes you can choose.             
     "Destination"       The destination you need to go to.        
     "now"               where you are now.                        
                                                                   
     "@"                 player position.                          
     "&"                 Enemies who encounter you will be reset to
                         original position.                        
     "E"                 Exits in the game.                        
     "."                 Walkable area.                            
     "w"                 Player moves up.                          
     "a"                 Player goes left.                         
     "s"                 Player goes down.                         
     "d"                 Player goes right.                        
                                                                   
     "Killer"            Killer location.                          
     "You"               Your location.
